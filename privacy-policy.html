Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Summary: WebWorthHub does not collect personal data to sell or share with third parties. We use Google AdSense and Google Analytics which may collect anonymized usage data as described below.

1. Who We Are

WebWorthHub.com operates the website at webworthhub.com. We provide a free website value estimation tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to operate our service. Specifically:

  • Usage data: Pages you visit, time spent on the site, browser type, and device type — collected anonymously via Google Analytics.
  • Search queries: Domain names you enter into our search tool. These are not linked to any personal identity.
  • Cookies: Small data files stored in your browser for remembering your preferences (e.g., dark mode setting, recently checked domains). These are stored locally on your device only.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used to:

  • Operate and improve the WebWorthHub service
  • Understand how visitors use our tool (via analytics)
  • Remember your preferences such as dark mode
  • Display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense

4. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to show ads based on your previous visits to our site and other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data from sites that use their services, visit: google.com/policies/privacy/partners

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects anonymized data such as your IP address (anonymized), browser, device, and pages visited. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Cookies

Our website uses the following types of cookies:

  • Preference cookies: Store your dark/light mode preference and recently checked domains. These never leave your browser.
  • Analytics cookies: Set by Google Analytics to track anonymized usage statistics.
  • Advertising cookies: Set by Google AdSense to display relevant ads.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect some features of the website.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense — advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Analytics — usage analytics (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts — typography (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Favicon API — website favicons in search results

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with anyone except as described in this policy (i.e., anonymized analytics data processed by Google).

9. Children's Privacy

WebWorthHub is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics and advertising cookies
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

11. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. Since we do not store personal information on our servers, your exposure is minimal. Your browser preferences (dark mode, recent searches) are stored locally on your own device.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: contact@webworthhub.com
  • Website: webworthhub.com
